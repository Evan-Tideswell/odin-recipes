Ramen

Description

A quick recipe to help get rid of all the unnecessary chemicals of normal top ramen, while keeping the flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups water
  • 1 (3 ounce) Package instant ramen noodle (exclude seasoning packet)
  • 2 Tablespoons Garlic Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons Onion Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons Ginger Powder
  • Salt and ground Black Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups water to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Cook noodles in boiling water until tender, about 3 minutes.
  3. Stir garlic powder, onion powder, and ginger into the noodles and water; season with salt and black pepper.
